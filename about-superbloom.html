About Us

Our goal is to be a contemproary, trendy, and community friendly shop that has unique products and services at an affordable price. Come stop by and say hello!

SuperBloom has everything from house plants, unique planting pots, gardening supplies, fresh flowers and florist services for events and weddings. Our shop was inspired by the super bloom that our owner, Courtney, visited in 2019 and was in awe by the miles and miles of beautiful yellow and orange flowers.
